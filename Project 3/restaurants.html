1.Lobster at Lorenzillo’s

The Riviera Maya is one of the best places in the world to sample lobster, and in Cancún, the most well-known destination for live lobster is Lorenzillo’s. This landmark restaurant has been Cancún’s lobster mothership for over 30 years; it even has a proprietary lobster farm. Lobster is served every which way — grilled, broiled, steamed — but if you want to go big, the jumbo lobster taco with rice, beans, and guacamole has your name on it.



2.Thai&Lounge




If you find yourself at La Isla Shopping Village, the convivial open-air shopping, eating, and entertainment venue which is also home to the Cancún Aquarium, head to Thai Lounge. With its private palapa-roofed dining huts overlooking the Nichupte Lagoon, it’s the perfect place to share a traditional Thai curry or noodle dish with friends. Make your way to the bar for the restaurant’s famous pre-dinner coconut martini, and watch the dolphins swimming in the next-door aquarium through the bar’s glass wall.
